Privacy Policy

We collect nothing unnecessary.

Effective date: May 17, 2026  ·  Applies to: Bright iputils (BrightDate-powered networking tools)

Bright iputils does not collect, store, transmit, or report any personal data, usage data, telemetry, diagnostics, or analytics — ever.

No account is required. No data leaves your device except for the explicit network packets sent as part of the tool's core function (e.g., ICMP echo requests for bping).

Bright iputils — BrightDate Networking Tools

Bright iputils is a suite of open-source networking tools (bping, bclockdiff, btracepath, barping) that operate entirely on-device, except for the network traffic you explicitly initiate (such as sending pings or ARP requests). These tools:

  • Do not collect or transmit any analytics, telemetry, or usage data.
  • Do not write data to disk except for output you explicitly redirect or save.
  • Do not require an account or registration.
  • Do not make network connections except as required for their core networking function (e.g., sending ICMP, ARP, or tracepath packets).
  • Do not access contacts, location, camera, microphone, or any other sensitive system resource.
  • Do not include any third-party analytics, crash-reporting, advertising, or data-collection services.

This Website

The Bright iputils project website (brightdate.org) may use third-party analytics (such as Google Analytics) to collect aggregate, anonymized page-view statistics (pages visited, approximate region, browser type). This data is governed by the respective third-party privacy policies. No personally identifiable information is collected by the website itself. Website analytics are entirely separate from the Bright iputils tools — the tools themselves have no connection to any website or analytics.

Third-Party Services

Bright iputils does not integrate with any third-party analytics, crash-reporting, advertising, or data-collection services. There are no in-app purchases, subscriptions, or accounts.

Children's Privacy

Because no data is collected at all, Bright iputils is safe for users of any age under applicable data-protection laws including COPPA and GDPR.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a new effective date. Given that our policy is "collect nothing", we do not anticipate substantive changes.

Contact

Questions about privacy can be directed to the project via GitHub Issues.
